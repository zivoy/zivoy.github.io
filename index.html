Animating

360° Videos

This is a 360° video instead of just rendering a camera view you render everything and let the viewer decide what they want to focus on.
360° Videos are movable (pause this video and look around)

Augmented reality

Agmented reality is motion tracked animations on a real video.
by tracking real points in the video the virtual camera can be synced with the real camera stabilising it allowing for the animations to look like there part of the video.

2D

Using compositing and shading you can make 3D models and animations seem as though they are 2D.
This allows for easy animating of seemingly 2D animations.
